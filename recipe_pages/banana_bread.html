Banana Bread

Description

Ingredients

  • 3 Overripe Bananas
  • 2 Cups GF Flower
  • 1/2 Cup Maple Syrup
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  • 3/4 Teaspoon Cinnamon
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Stick of Butter

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients
  2. Using potato masher, mash bananas until they form a liquid
  3. Melt butter, add to mashed bananas
  4. Add vanilla extract to wet mixture
  5. Scramble eggs and add to wet mixture
  6. Add maple syrup to wet mixture
  7. Combine wet ingredients to dry, mix until completely combined
  8. Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes
