Steak

Description

Ingredients

  • Steak, 1/4 lb to 3/4 lb
  • Butter
  • Rosemary
  • Salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Heat cast iron pan, add butter to coat
  2. Season steak generously with salt and pepper
  3. Sear steak on pan, with rosemary placed on top
  4. Halfway through the searing, add butter generously and baste
  5. Let rest 5 minutes and serve
